Brand Guidelines

Visual identity and brand standards for consistent communication

Voice & Tone

Brand Personality: Professional yet approachable, confident without being arrogant, helpful and solution-focused

✓ DO

  • • Use clear, jargon-free language
  • • Focus on customer benefits
  • • Be specific with metrics (33% savings)
  • • Show regional expertise
  • • Use active voice

✗ DON'T

  • • Use technical jargon unnecessarily
  • • Make unsupported claims
  • • Sound overly sales-focused
  • • Ignore local Arkansas advantage
  • • Use passive voice
