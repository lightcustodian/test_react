3. **Accountability** - _Integrity/Trust_ - We are open and honest. The best interests of our team, clients, and partners are our number one priority. What is measured improves, but what is measured and reported improves dramatically.

We seek to provide all members of our team an opportunity to lead in whatever capacity they are capable and interested, and to increase that capacity as they grow in our organization.
